8051 Development Board

8051 Development Board

This development board developed by our team ‘Wizminds Tech’ to conduct workshops forÂ students of our college who were eager to learn basic robotics skills (but later we took it to teach other college students as well). Various sensors can be connected to the microcontroller directly or to the op-amp on board. Here are the features of the board:

1. Microcontroller: AT89s52

2. OpAmp: Volatage comparator (LM324)

3. Current Driver: L293D (Max. driving current 1 amp)

4. Bridge Rectifier and volatage regulator

5. In-circuit programming
